The Model

My tumor segmentation model shown above is a U-Net, and was built using Pytorch. The input images were first reshaped into standardized 224x224x1 grayscale arrays with augmentations, they were then normalized through positive global standardization. After being trained on the data, the model segments on images. Below are a few of my model's training parameters and metrics:

Initial Learning Rate: 0.001
Number of Total Training Epochs: 40
Final Test Set Accuracy: 0.85
